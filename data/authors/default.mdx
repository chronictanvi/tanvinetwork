## About
<h3 class="prose text-xl md:text-3xl max-w-35ch"> Tanvi is a design technologist operating at the intersection of data, aesthetics, & the humanities. </h3>
<p class="md:text-lg text-base"> Currently, she is a Freelance Art Director at <a href="https://www.apple.com/" target="_blank">Apple.</a> She is a co-author of <cite><a href="https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262056182/data-by-design/" target="_blank">Data by Design: A History in Five Charts</a></cite> (MIT Press, 2026). Previously, she has worked at Pentagram, Public Policy Lab, and Spotify. She is based in Harlem with her two cats — Yuchi and Bubbles.</p>
