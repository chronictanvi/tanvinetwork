## About
<h3 class="prose text-xl md:text-3xl max-w-35ch"> Tanvi is a design technologist operating at the intersection of data, aesthetics, & the humanities. </h3>
<p class="md:text-lg text-base"> She works as a is a design engineer  at Atlanta Interdisciplinary AI Network, BIPOC Design History and is a co-author of <cite>Data by Design: A History in Five Charts </cite> (MIT Press, 2026).  She is also a Visiting Professor at Pratt Institute. Previously, she worked at  Pentagram, Public Policy Lab, and Spotify. She is based in Brooklyn with her two cats — Yuchi and Bubbles. </p>
